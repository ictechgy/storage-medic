Storage Medic Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Storage Medic is a macOS storage management app that helps users scan selected folders and review cleanup candidates.

Data Collection

Storage Medic does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data through the app.

The app does not use analytics, advertising trackers, third-party tracking SDKs, or remote user profiling.

File Access

Storage Medic scans only folders selected by the user. Scan results are processed locally on the user’s Mac.

Storage Medic does not upload file names, file contents, scan results, or cleanup history to external servers.

Cleanup

Storage Medic shows cleanup candidates before any cleanup action. Confirmed cleanup items are moved to Trash by default.

Sensitive Locations

Storage Medic is designed to exclude sensitive locations such as iCloud, Photos, Keychain, Mail, Messages, and FileProvider from cleanup candidates.

Support Contact

If you contact us for support through GitHub Issues, we may receive the information you choose to provide, such as your GitHub username, message, screenshots, macOS version, or app version. This information is used only to respond to your request and improve support.

GitHub may process information you provide on GitHub according to GitHub’s own privacy policy and terms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy when Storage Medic’s data practices or support process changes. The updated date at the top of this page will reflect the latest version.
